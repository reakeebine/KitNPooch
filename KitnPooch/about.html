Who are we?

Kit n Pooch is a South African online pet store based in Cape Town although we do international deliveries too. We pride ourselves on providing luxury pet apparel and healthy pet food to cater to your extra special kit or pooch. We also are a hub for informative updates about pets.

Our call centre is open every day of the year for queries or assistance!
